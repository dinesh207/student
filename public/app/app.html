ABOUT US

This web-application was designed, coded, and marketed by Varun Bhardwaj and Harsha Valluri in order to connects students in need of service hours willing to tutor with students in need of tutoring. Our service hours are provided by the homework help center at South Brunswick High School. We offer tutoring in various subjects in addition to standardized testing such as SAT and ACT. We hope you find our application useful. If you contact us if you have any issues at instatutorcontact@gmail.com.
